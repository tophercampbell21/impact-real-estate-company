What Is Impact Real Estate Company?

Impact Real Estate Company is a locally owned and operated family-run home buying business. We specialize in helping homeowners like you sell their properties quickly and hassle-free. Our process is safe and simple which allows homeowners to sell their property without paying commissions or hidden fees, or making costly repairs to the property. Our team of experts is committed to making the selling process as smooth and stress-free as possible for you. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you sell your home quickly and easily.

Our Process
It's as easy as Accept → Sign → Sell

01Accept

After studying the comparables in your neighborhood and quickly assessing the property's condition we'll be ready to make you our offer. You'll recieve this fair and competitive offer for your property in as little as the same day!

02Sign

If the offer matches your expectations and you want to move forward, we can sign it up and start moving to closing. Our team of experts will guide you through the sale of your property all the way to the end.

03Sell

In as little as two weeks, you will have completed the sale of your property without paying commissions or doing expensive repairs or renovations. And if you need help finding a new home while selling this one, we can help with that too!
